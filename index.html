Privacy Policy

Pulse Music Player

Last Updated: March 4, 2026

Introduction

Pulse Music Player ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Pulse Music Player is designed to work completely offline on your device.

Local Data Only

The app accesses the following information locally on your device:

  • Audio files stored on your device (with your permission)
  • App settings and preferences
  • Playlist data and favorites
  • Listening history and statistics
  • Custom equalizer settings

All this data remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Permissions

The app requires the following permissions to function:

  • Storage/Media Access: To read and play your audio files
  • Foreground Service: To continue playing music when the app is in the background
  • Wake Lock: To prevent the device from sleeping during playback
  • Internet Access: Only for displaying ads in the free version and processing in-app purchases
  • Notifications: To show playback controls and current song information

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob (Free Version Only)

The free version of the app displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data according to their privacy policy:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • IP address
  • Advertising ID

View Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Premium users do not see ads and AdMob does not collect any data.

Google Play Billing

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed through Google Play Billing. Google may collect payment information according to their privacy policy. We do not have access to your payment details.

Data Storage

  • All app data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not operate any servers or databases
  • Your music files and listening data never leave your device
  • You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app

Analytics

We do not use any analytics or tracking services. We do not monitor your usage, collect crash reports, or track your behavior in any way.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and does not contain age-inappropriate content.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store your personal information, there is no data to access, modify, or delete on our end. All app data is controlled by you on your device.

You can:

  • Clear app data through your device settings
  • Revoke permissions at any time
  • Uninstall the app to remove all data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, security is managed by your device's security features. We recommend:

  • Using a secure lock screen
  • Keeping your device's operating system up to date
  • Only installing apps from trusted sources

International Users

This app can be used worldwide. Since we do not collect or transmit data, there are no international data transfer concerns.
